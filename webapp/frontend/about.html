About

Storygraph is a hackathon project that allows you to visualize stories. It tries to understand the characters, descriptions, and tone in your story by constructing a knowledge graph for each sentence you speak. The vision is to eventually utilize the same technology to visualize interactive storytelling in a virtual reality environment.
